Beyond making things look visually appealing, my projects reflect everything from research, experimentation, and iteration to get a better view on what is important to the user.

UX Research

With design research, designs are more meaningful and effective because they are grounded in a real-world context. My intention is to put this analysis together in a clean, cohesive manner that provides cohesiveness, alignment and shared understanding with my team.

Content Strategy

Content strategy is about planning and strategizing for useful and usable content. Through the analysis, prioritization and strategization of content, it will be easier to create better user experiences.

Prototyping

Sketching, wireframing and prototyping process, through sketching and wireframing, and creating prototypes is a cost effective way to investigate and solve minor nuiances with the app, and allow for YUGE improvements without the need for expensive programming. This saves time, money, and headache having to go back making changes that could have been solved during the design process.

Visual Design

Visual design is more than just making things look "pretty". Incorporating images and forms with the purpose of improving communication and usability can make a big impact on the usability ad functionality of your product. Color, typography, shapes and images are the visual tools I use to improve user experience and usability.
